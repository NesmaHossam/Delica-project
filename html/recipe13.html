GRILLED FISH

When you are in a rush or just dont feel like doing much for dinner, grilled fish is a perfect choice. Making this recipe using a skillet, a grill pan, or an indoor grill such as the George Foreman and some yummy fish seasoning takes no time at all but it sure does taste like you spent hours prepping. This fish is delicious when served with steamed broccoli on the side. Or if you have a little more time, try it with my Pan Fried Parmesan Zucchini or Roasted Asparagus and a light Peach and Blueberry Parfait for dessert.
