CHICKEN(MANDI)

Tip #1: Buy whole spices, toast them, and grind them yourself. This will make a huge difference in the flavour of both the chicken and rice. And its easy! Tip #2: Be patient with the onions while they fry. The onions is what really takes this dish to another level of umami. Just keep an eye on them and plan accordingly. Tip #3: Don't be scared of using the quantity of oil used in the recipe. Many store bough Mandi uses even more oil than this. The oil will help keep the rice grains soft and luxurious.
